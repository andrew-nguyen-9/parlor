# Parlor Design Document

# Ignite

*A daily alchemical ritual where ancient runes, elemental chemistry, and living flame converge. Every symbol carries meaning. Every reaction follows immutable rules. Only the correct spell can awaken the Eternal Flame.*

---

# Overview

**Ignite** is one of the daily games in the **Parlor** collection (`parlor.an9.dev`).

Each Parlor game explores a different style of logic. Ignite explores **symbolic reasoning, alchemy, elemental interactions, and logical deduction**.

Every day, players are presented with a dormant ritual circle deep within an ancient forest shrine. Candles remain extinguished. Arcane runes lie dormant. Alchemical ingredients wait to be combined. The ritual has been partially prepared, but something is missing.

The objective is not simply to light every candle.

The objective is to understand the ritual itself.

Players must decipher relationships between runes, elemental affinities, chemical compounds, magical constraints, and ritual sequencing until the spell becomes self-sustaining and the Eternal Flame awakens.

Every rune influences another.

Every element changes the behavior of fire.

Every decision has visible magical consequences.

The experience should feel mystical, premium, atmospheric, and deeply satisfying. There are no timers, no random guessing, and no hidden information. Players succeed through observation, deduction, experimentation, and understanding increasingly sophisticated magical systems.

The defining emotional moment is:

> "The final candle ignites... the ritual circle awakens... and the entire shrine comes alive."

## Ancient Alchemy

Ignite blends fantasy with real-world science.

Rather than inventing fictional chemistry, the game uses authentic chemical compounds, elemental properties, combustion principles, oxidation, catalysts, minerals, salts, metals, and molecular structures as inspiration for puzzles.

Completing a puzzle should feel like performing both science and sorcery simultaneously.

# Gameplay

Every daily ritual consists of:

* One ritual circle
* Existing rune set from the current Ignite implementation
* Candles
* Alchemical ingredients
* One deterministic solution
* One magical simulation

Players can:

Arrange runes

Rotate magical glyphs

Move candles

Mix ingredients

Reveal elemental clues

Observe reactions

Trigger the ritual

Run the simulation

If the ritual is correct:

Candles ignite one by one.

Runes glow.

Magic spreads across engraved channels.

Roots beneath the shrine illuminate.

Ancient spirits briefly awaken.

The Eternal Flame erupts.

The puzzle completes.

---

# Core Puzzle Mechanics

The existing rune system remains the foundation of Ignite and should be expanded rather than replaced.

Possible mechanics include:

- Rune adjacency affects magical resonance.
- Certain runes amplify or suppress neighboring symbols.
- Candles only ignite when their required rune conditions are satisfied.
- Fire propagates according to logical elemental pathways.
- Alchemical compounds modify ritual behavior.
- Catalysts unlock otherwise impossible reactions.
- Certain runes require activation order.
- Ritual energy follows visible engraved channels.
- Some magical reactions are reversible while others permanently alter the board.

Every mechanic should remain deterministic and understandable.

# Claude Implementation Priorities

1. Preserve and expand the existing rune system rather than replacing it, building every mechanic around those established symbols.
2. Redesign the visual experience into a flagship-quality ancient forest shrine with premium CSS, cinematic lighting, atmospheric particles, layered animations, and highly polished interactions.
3. Develop a deterministic ritual engine where runes, candles, elemental affinities, alchemical compounds, and fire propagation obey reusable logical rules that always produce exactly one valid solution.
4. Deeply integrate real chemistry through compounds, molecular structures, combustion science, and educational Wikipedia content unlocked after each completed puzzle.
5. Implement a procedural daily puzzle generator that scales naturally from approachable Monday rituals to Sunday's Master Ritual while ensuring fairness, replay consistency, and increasing strategic depth.
6. Expand the Compendium into a collectible archive of runes, compounds, herbs, minerals, and scientific discoveries that rewards long-term play without affecting puzzle difficulty.
7. Optimize rendering with modern Three.js techniques, premium shader work, GPU particle systems, realistic flame effects, responsive performance, and seamless integration into the broader Parlor ecosystem.

Ignite should become the visual and emotional flagship of Parlor. Players should finish every session remembering one unforgettable moment: standing before an ancient ritual circle as the final candle catches, the runes blaze with impossible light, and centuries of forgotten magic awaken through a system they understood—not by chance, but through logic.
