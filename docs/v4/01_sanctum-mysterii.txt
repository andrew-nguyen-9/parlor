# Parlor Design Document

# Sanctum Mysterii

*A daily investigation where every clue has a purpose, every suspect guards a secret, and every contradiction hides another truth. Wealth, deception, magic, and murder have haunted the halls of Sanctum Manor for generations. Only careful deduction can expose the killer.*

---

# Overview

**Sanctum Mysterii** is the flagship mystery experience in the **Parlor** collection (`parlor.an9.dev`).

Sanctum Mysterii is a **daily deterministic murder mystery** built around logical deduction. Every case presents a new crime with a unique cast of suspects, relationships, motives, locations, evidence, witness statements, and timelines. **The core game should remain grounded in deduction rather than narrative branching or random outcomes.** This document focuses on improving puzzle architecture, balancing difficulty, elevating the visual presentation, refining the user experience, and expanding the technical systems that support a premium detective experience.

Unlike traditional detective games that rely on intuition or hidden information, every mystery in Sanctum Mysterii must be solvable through observation, logic, contradiction, and careful organization of evidence. Players should feel like world-class detectives—not because the game tells them they are, but because every conclusion is earned.

This should become the narrative centerpiece of Parlor, quietly connecting the lore, families, artifacts, and mysteries scattered across the other games without ever requiring players to understand the larger universe.

The defining emotional moment is:

> *"Every suspect has spoken... every contradiction has been resolved... and the final accusation reveals the only version of events that could ever have been true."*

# Improving Puzzle Logic

The existing mysteries sometimes fluctuate between being immediately obvious and becoming frustratingly difficult.

Rather than increasing complexity, puzzles should improve through **layered reasoning.**

---

## Multi-Layer Evidence

Every case should gradually build toward certainty.

Instead of presenting isolated clues, evidence should naturally reinforce previous discoveries.

Example layers:

Witness Statement

↓

Physical Evidence

↓

Relationship

↓

Timeline

↓

Motive

↓

Contradiction

↓

Final Conclusion

Every deduction should unlock confidence rather than confusion.

---

## Progressive Information Architecture

Rather than overwhelming players immediately:

Early clues establish facts.

Middle clues introduce contradictions.

Late clues resolve ambiguity.

Difficulty comes from combining information rather than remembering large amounts of text.

## Truth & Deception System

Witnesses may:

Tell the truth.

Misremember events.

Lie intentionally.

Withhold information.

The important distinction:

The game always provides enough evidence for players to identify unreliable testimony.

Examples:

"This witness has lied previously."

"The butler never lies."

"The twins always protect one another."

"The medium cannot distinguish dreams from reality."

Players are solving credibility as much as the crime itself.

Rather than building isolated UI components, Sanctum Mysterii should introduce reusable investigative systems that can evolve with future mysteries.

---

Supports:

Relationship boards

Maps

Evidence walls

Timelines

Family trees

# Rendering & Performance Architecture

The experience should remain visually rich while targeting smooth performance across a wide range of devices.

## Layered Rendering Strategy

Separate rendering into independent visual layers:

Background environment

Ambient lighting

Environmental particles

Interactive UI

Investigation overlays

Animated effects

Only changed layers should re-render.

---

## JavaScript Optimization

Implement:

RequestAnimationFrame scheduling

Animation batching

Intersection Observer

Virtualized evidence lists

Memoized relationship graphs

Lazy-loaded investigation modules

Object pooling for particles

GPU-friendly transforms

Minimal layout recalculations

Progressive enhancement based on device capability

Maintain smooth interaction even on complex Sunday mysteries.

---

## Adaptive Quality System

Automatically scale:

Particle density

Shadow quality

Blur intensity

Reflection layers

Lighting effects

Animation frequency

Environmental simulations

High-end devices receive cinematic presentation.

Older devices retain identical functionality with simplified effects.

---

## Motion Architecture

Use lightweight CSS transitions for interface interactions.

Reserve GSAP or React Spring for:

Evidence organization

Relationship animations

Timeline movement

Case completion

Camera transitions

Avoid unnecessary JavaScript animations for static UI.

# Claude Implementation Priorities

1. Preserve the deterministic murder mystery structure while improving difficulty through layered deduction, progressive clue ordering, richer evidence types, and stronger logical reinforcement instead of increasing raw complexity.
2. Transform Sanctum Mysterii into Parlor's flagship detective experience with a luxurious Victorian aesthetic inspired by *Knives Out*, *Clue*, historic manor houses, occult mysteries, and private investigative studies.
3. Build a modular investigation architecture capable of dynamically presenting relationship boards, timelines, estate maps, evidence walls, family trees, and detective notebooks depending on each day's mystery without overwhelming the player.
4. Extend the existing Parlor design system with reusable detective components, premium typography, leather and parchment materials, wax seals, brass accents, and elegant interaction patterns that maximize readability and reduce cognitive load across desktop and mobile.
5. Implement a high-performance rendering pipeline using layered UI rendering, adaptive quality scaling, memoized graph structures, GPU-accelerated animations, virtualized data views, and progressive enhancement to maintain fluid performance on every device.
6. Quietly position Sanctum Mysterii as the narrative heart of the Parlor universe by weaving recurring families, hidden organizations, magical artifacts, manor history, and environmental storytelling into daily investigations while ensuring every mystery remains self-contained, logically fair, and deeply satisfying to solve.

Sanctum Mysterii should become the game players think of when they imagine Parlor. Not because it tells the biggest story, but because every day they step into the same magnificent manor, uncover another impossible crime, and realize that beneath every solved case lies another secret waiting patiently within the walls.
